Friends of Barham Library

Unit 4, 660 Harrow Road, Wembley, London, HA0 2HB

Barham Community Library is based in the Barham Park building on the frontage to Harrow Road. It is run by volunteers and replaces the library closed by Brent Council in October 2011. The full address is Unit 4, 660 Harrow Road Wembley HA0 2HB and buses 18, 92, 182, 204 and H17 stop nearby.
