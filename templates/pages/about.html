About

Barham Community Library is a volunteer-run community library based in Wembley, UK. It is operated by Friends of Barham Library, a registered charity, and continues thanks to the time, care and enthusiasm of local volunteers.

More than a book collection, the library is also a community space for activities, events and room hire.

For opening hours, activities, shop details and the latest updates please visit the Friends of Barham Library website.
