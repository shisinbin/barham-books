About the library

Friends of Barham Library is a volunteer-run community library based in Wembley, UK. The library is operated by a registered charity and has been serving the local community since 2011. Today, it continues thanks to the time, care and enthusiasm of its volunteers.

This website focuses primarily on the library's general book collection, making it easier to explore what's on the shelves and discover new reads.

Beyond the books

Barham Library is more than just a place to borrow or browse books. It is an active community space, hosting a wide range of activities and events, and offering the library as a venue for one-off or regular hires.
