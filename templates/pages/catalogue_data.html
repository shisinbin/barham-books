About the file

This dataset includes selected information about books in the catalogue, including titles, authors, descriptions, tags, categories, series details and original publication years where available.

It may be useful for developers, researchers, book enthusiasts or anyone interested in exploring the collection in a structured format.

Notes on the data

The catalogue has been built and maintained by volunteers, with book information checked and refined from a range of sources including the Google Books API, Open Library, publisher websites and other book-related resources.

{% comment %}

The file is a maintained snapshot rather than a live API. Some records may contain gaps, corrections-in-progress or catalogue-specific editorial choices.

{% endcomment %}
